Mr. Vinod Modha

Vinod Modha, a CA with a B.Com background, is the Founder Partner at KMS Strategic Advisors LLP. With a career spanning since 1981, he excels in legal-commercial advisory, business structuring, succession planning, and fintech strategy. His expertise extends across various industries, including finance, infrastructure, manufacturing, services, and technology. Additionally, he serves on the Advisory Boards and Boards of Directors for multiple corporate entities, including multinational corporations.

Mr. Himanshu Shah

Himanshu Shah is a seasoned finance expert with a remarkable 25-year career in the industry. He held a key position as Assistant Vice President at Claris Lifesciences, a leading pharmaceutical company, where he significantly impacted the financial landscape. His expertise spans various financial aspects, including loans, fund raising, risk management, and cross-functional collaboration. He has transitioned into a project finance consultant role while maintaining his dedication to financial excellence and adaptability in the finance sector.

Mr. Mehul Dhokai

With over 22 years of extensive management expertise, Mehul Dhokai brings a wealth of experience to the table. With a notable corporate tenure spanning over 17 years, he has made significant contributions in various organizations. He has held esteemed positions on the boards of renowned companies such as Anand Rathi, Kotak, Angel Broking, Shell, and Gillette. In 2017, Mehul Dhokai embarked on a new journey by establishing FundBuddy. Under his leadership, this venture has not only made investments but also successfully nurtured and expanded startups in sectors like AdTech, AgriTech, and CleanTech.
