Rakesh Patel

Rakesh Bhailalbhai Patel is a Gujarati business pioneer who is the head of very much popular BD Patel group of companies settled in Ahmedabad, India.

Mr. Rakesh Patel currently has over 40 years of expanded insight in multiple activities.

He is a sequential business visionary and conveys a demonstrated history of dealing with and growing various business lines.

He is an extremely far-located and visionary money manager, who can guide and lead the group in an exceptionally successful way.

Rakesh Lahoti

Rakesh Lahoti is a distinguished Chartered Accountant whose expertise transcends conventional boundaries. His comprehensive understanding of finance extends to visionary realms like e-commerce, as evidenced by his diploma from Nirma Institution in Ahmedabad. His prescience regarding the Dot Com bubble's impending burst showcased his foresight in the volatile tech industry.

Over a remarkable journey spanning more than two decades, Rakesh Lahoti's unwavering determination to scale the pinnacle of success has been evident. Throughout this tenure, he's been an eyewitness to pivotal financial events, from the soaring heights and catastrophic downfall of figures like Harshad Mehta to grappling with crises such as the infamous KP scam. His journey has been marked by navigating the intricate landscape of political milestones, the tumultuous saga of ILFs, and even weathering the market-shaking tremors brought on by the COVID-19 pandemic.

Lahoti's academic prowess and professional journey further attest to his multifaceted skills. As a Chartered Accountant, he holds a myriad of certifications, including from the National Institute of Securities Market (NISM) and a Certification Course on Forensic Accounting & Fraud Detection (FAFD) from ICAI. His active involvement as Chairman of the Information Technology Committee and various leadership roles in ICAI's Western India Regional Council showcases his commitment to digitization and advancing the profession's technology aspect.

His global outlook is reflected in his involvement in studying and participating in international capital market conferences across ASEAN countries, including Singapore, Hong Kong, Korea, Thailand, China, and Malaysia. Additionally, Lahoti has shared his expertise by delivering lectures on Derivatives and Capital Markets across diverse platforms, solidifying his status as an authority in the financial domain. His journey resonates with profound insight, resilience, and an unwavering commitment to making an enduring impact on the landscape of success.
